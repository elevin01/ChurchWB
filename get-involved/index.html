Join Us

IGC NYC is a community that is being continually shaped by the Holy Spirit. We meet near Middletown Road (1621 Pilgrim Ave) in the Bronx to worship God, study Scripture, learn and grow in faith together, love our neighbors, nurture friendships, and serve our community.

Our shared life is formed through ongoing rhythms of worship, learning, prayer, breaking of bread, fellowship, generosity and witness.
