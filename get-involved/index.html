Join Us

IGC NYC is a community that is being continually shaped by the Holy Spirit. We meet near Middletown Road (1621 Pilgrim Ave) in the Bronx to worship God, study Scripture, learn and grow in faith together, love our neighbors, nurture friendships, and serve our community.

Our shared life is formed through ongoing rhythms of worship, learning, prayer, breaking of bread, fellowship, generosity and witness.

Ways to be part of our community life

Sunday

Sunday School

We are currently studying Fundamentals of the Faith: 13 Lessons to Grow in the Grace and Knowledge of Jesus Christ by John MacArthur.

This 13-lesson study covers essential Christian truths - from God's character to the purpose of the Church - helping believers build a strong biblical foundation and grow in their walk with Christ.

Ideal for new believers or anyone seeking to deepen their faith.

Time: Every Sunday @ 3:00 PM - 3:30 PM

Sunday

Worship Service

The worship service follows from 3:30 and usually lasts about 60-90 minutes. During our time together, we sing songs of praise in different languages, share concerns and joys for communal prayer, listen to Scripture in Spanish and English, and receive Bible teaching and communion.

A fellowship meal is held after the service. Whether you are new to faith or have been walking with Christ for years, you are welcome to join us as we worship, learn, and serve together.

Time: Every Sunday @ 3:30 PM (60-90 min)

Ministries

Table Cafe

Table Cafe meets every Saturday and is designed as an open door to our community. It's a welcoming space for neighbors, friends, and newcomers to gather for conversation, coffee, and connection.

Whether you're exploring faith, new to the area, or simply looking for community, Table Cafe offers a relaxed environment to ask questions, build relationships, and experience the warmth of IGC NYC.

Time: Every Saturday @ 10:30 AM - 12:00 PM
