About our church

Rooted here, present here.

Bronx Community Church is a local church shaped by everyday people. We care about showing up, listening first, and staying close to the neighborhood we serve.

I grew up on these blocks and know the strength and pressure our neighbors carry. My calling is to be a steady presence, to listen well, and to help people find hope in Jesus without pretense or pressure.

You will find me on the street, at the table, or praying with someone who needs a hand. We are not here for a spotlight. We are here for people.

Our identity

Values that shape how we show up.

We show up.

We stay close to our neighbors, especially when life feels heavy.

We serve before we speak.

Care comes first. Trust is built over time, not through slogans.

We believe the gospel changes whole lives.

Hope is personal and practical, for people and neighborhoods.

We work with the community.

Partnerships matter. We are here alongside, not above.

What we believe

We believe Jesus meets people with compassion, invites us into forgiveness and new life, and calls us to love our neighbors in real ways. We keep our faith centered on grace and lived out through service.
