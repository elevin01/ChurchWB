Who We Are

Equipping people to be like Jesus.

We believe that following Jesus makes life better for you, your family, and our community. It does not make life perfect, but it makes life make more sense.

Whether you have been in church your whole life or never stepped foot in one, our aim is to create a safe place for everyone to be all that God has intended them to be.

On this page you can learn more about us, who we are, and what we are all about. We believe God wants something for you, not something from you. And we want the same.

Please reach out if we can help. We are a neighborhood church, and we will walk with you at a steady pace.

Family Church Story

A place to call home in the Bronx.

You are searching for a church to call home. A place filled with real, compassionate people who welcome you with open arms. A place where your kids look forward to going, and you do too.

Bronx Community Church is such a place. Here you will find the connection, community, and comfort you crave. We know how difficult it can be to find a home church that lovingly leads you closer to Jesus, so we keep a simple, real-life approach to all things church.

We focus on sharing biblical truth, participating in daily discipleship, modeling obedience, engaging in meaningful gatherings, and serving generously. We aim to be humble and real: we want something for you, not from you.

Life without a strong church community can feel lonely, chaotic, and empty. We want more for you than that. Together, we can build stronger families, healthier relationships, and happier lives while stepping into the fullness of all God designed us to be. At Bronx Community Church, community means family.

Core Values

This is what we want to be known as.

These are our core values, the way we want to live together.

Unconditionally loving

We see each person's God-given value and strive to love all as Christ loves us.

John 13:34-35

Selflessly generous

We actively seek opportunities to give our time, talent, and treasures to advance the kingdom of God.

1 John 3:16-18

Intimacy driven

We are driven by our relationship with the Father. He is the source of life and shapes who we are.

John 15:4

Humble and real

We will be open, teachable, and genuine in all interactions, the same at church as everywhere else.

James 2:14

Fearlessly obedient

Through a consistent commitment to God's word and voice, we receive direction and follow it with all we are.

John 14:23

Faithful farmers

With patience and grace, we come alongside everyone and lovingly lead them toward Jesus.

Matthew 9:35-38

Intentional equippers

By teaching people how to use the Word and walk fully in their gifts, we give them freedom to live out their calling.

1 Timothy 3:16

Joyfully loyal

We are committed to the great commission and invest our time, resources, and energy to bring hurting souls closer to Jesus.

Matthew 28:19
